How I Start My Journey in Computer Science

My journey into computer science began when I first get the chance to learn programming during my second trimester in Foundation in Science studies in Universiti Tunku Abdul Rahman (UTAR). I was interested with programming like how I can use code to tell computer to perform task for me and solve many real life problems. Programming also required me to think logically and I liked it when I tried to think logically and deeply, I get to solve a complex problems. I was initially planning to take Biology-related degree programmes but I then discovered that I liked Biology but I did not have interests to study Biology-related courses in depth. Hence, I have conducted research and find many information about Computer Science and finally I decided to transfer my degree program from Biotechnology to Computer Science in my third year in Foundation studies.The images below showed that I was studying Foundation in Science with major in science stream subjects which include Biology, Chemistry and Physics.
