Volleyball

One of my favourite sports is playing volleyball. I usually played volleyball with my friends at least once a week. Through playing volleyball, I have recognised many friends who always help me when I was in difficulties and have fun with me by travelling, playing volleyball and badminton, having breakfast, lunch and dinner together, chatting and more. Sometimes me and my friends even went to the stadium in Ipoh to play volleyball. I like volleyball because it is a sports that requires teamwork and collaboration and it is fun to play with my friends together.

Badminton

My another favourite sports is playing badminton. I usually played badminton at night and evening. I played badminton since I was in primary school. I really enjoyed playing badminton with my friends and after badminton session, we will usually go for dinner or supper and chatting with friends. Playing badminton with my friends fosters the relationship between me and my friends, relieves my stress, allows me to have better sleep quality and keeps me healthy and fit.
